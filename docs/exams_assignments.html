Homework Assignments

There will be 5 homework assignments, and a HW 0 that you complete to set up your environment. You will find information on HW 0 on CourseWorks and below when we publish. Homework assignments are a combination of written questions reinforcing critical concepts and practical exercises. The practical exercises requires using databases, e.g. database queries, designing schema, etc.

Students have a total of 5 late submission grace days:

  • Home work assignments are due at 11:59 PM on the date the assignment is due.
  • One minute past the deadline counts as a full late date. You accrue one additional day for every 24 hours.
  • You can apply as many remaining late days that you have to any homework assignment.
  • Once you have used all late days, a late assignment gets a 0.
